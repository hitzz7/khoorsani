Chilli Noodle Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack Noodles
  • 2 tbsp Chilli Oil
  • 1 tbsp Mustard Oil
  • 1 tsp Garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp Ginger (grated)
  • 1 tsp Soy Sauce
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper

Instructions:

1. Boil the noodles as per the package instructions and drain.

2. Heat oil in a pan, add garlic and stir-fry vegetables.

3. Add the noodles, chilli oil, soy sauce, salt, and pepper.

4. Toss everything together and stir-fry for 2 minutes.

5. Serve hot and enjoy!

Chowmin

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack Noodles
  • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1/2 cup Vegetables (carrot, peas, beans, etc.)
  • 1 tsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper

Instructions:

1. Boil the noodles and set them aside.

2. Heat oil in a pan and add vegetables, stir-fry until soft.

3. Add boiled noodles, soy sauce, salt, and pepper.

4. Stir-fry everything for 2-3 minutes.

5. Serve hot and enjoy!

Thaklai Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Rice
  • 1 tbsp Ghee
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
  • Water

Instructions:

1. Wash rice thoroughly and drain.

2. Heat ghee in a pan and add cumin seeds.

3. Add rice, salt, turmeric powder, black pepper, and water.

4. Cook on low heat until the rice is tender and water is absorbed.

5. Fluff and serve hot!
